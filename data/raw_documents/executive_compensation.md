## Overview
This document outlines the compensation structure for executive-level positions within the organization.

## Base Salary Structure

Executive-level salaries are structured as follows:
- Chief Executive Officer: $850,000 base salary
- Chief Financial Officer: $650,000 base salary  
- Chief Technology Officer: $600,000 base salary
- Chief Operating Officer: $620,000 base salary

## Performance Bonuses

Annual bonuses are calculated as a percentage of base salary:
- Target bonus: 75% of base salary
- Maximum bonus: 150% of base salary
- Minimum bonus: 0% (in case of underperformance)

Performance metrics include:
1. Revenue growth (40% weight)
2. Profit margin improvement (30% weight)
3. Strategic initiative completion (20% weight)
4. Employee satisfaction scores (10% weight)

## Equity Compensation

All executive officers receive stock options as follows:
- Initial grant: 100,000 options vesting over 4 years
- Annual refresh: 25,000 options per year
- Strike price: Fair market value at grant date

## Benefits Package

Executives receive enhanced benefits:
- Premium health insurance (100% employer-paid)
- $2 million life insurance policy
- Executive retirement plan (401k match up to $30,000 annually)
- Annual wellness allowance: $10,000
- Professional development budget: $15,000

## Severance Provisions

In case of termination without cause:
- 12 months base salary
- Accelerated vesting of 50% unvested equity
- 6 months continuation of health benefits

This policy is reviewed annually by the Compensation Committee.
