## Welcome

Welcome to our organization. This handbook provides essential information about your employment.

## Working Hours

Standard working hours:
- Monday to Friday: 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM
- 1-hour lunch break (unpaid)
- Total: 40 hours per week

Flexible work arrangements are available subject to manager approval.

## Paid Time Off (PTO)

All full-time employees receive:
- Year 1: 15 days PTO
- Years 2-5: 20 days PTO  
- Years 6+: 25 days PTO

PTO accrues monthly and can be used for vacation, sick leave, or personal time.

## Holidays

The company observes 10 paid holidays per year:
- New Year's Day
- Memorial Day
- Independence Day
- Labor Day
- Thanksgiving (2 days)
- Christmas (2 days)
- 2 Floating holidays (employee's choice)

## Health Benefits

Full-time employees are eligible for:
- Medical insurance (employee pays 20% premium)
- Dental insurance (employee pays 30% premium)
- Vision insurance (employee pays 30% premium)
- Basic life insurance ($50,000 coverage, employer-paid)

## 401(k) Retirement Plan

- Employees can contribute up to IRS limits
- Company matches 50% of employee contributions up to 6% of salary
- Vesting schedule: 100% vested after 3 years of service

## Professional Development

Employees have access to:
- LinkedIn Learning courses
- Annual conference attendance (with approval)
- Tuition reimbursement up to $5,000 per year for job-related courses

## Code of Conduct

All employees must:
- Treat colleagues with respect
- Maintain confidentiality of company information
- Report conflicts of interest
- Comply with all applicable laws and regulations

## Performance Reviews

Annual performance reviews are conducted in December. Reviews assess:
- Job performance against objectives
- Skill development
- Contribution to team goals
