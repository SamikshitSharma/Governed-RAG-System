MANAGEMENT POLICIES AND PROCEDURES
Version 1.5 - Confidential - Authority: Management

PERFORMANCE REVIEW PROCESS

Annual performance reviews are conducted every December. Managers must complete reviews for all direct reports.

Review Components:
1. Self-assessment submitted by employee
2. Manager evaluation against objectives
3. 360-degree feedback from peers (optional)
4. Performance rating: Exceeds Expectations, Meets Expectations, Needs Improvement, Unsatisfactory

Performance Improvement Plans (PIP):
Employees rated as "Needs Improvement" must be placed on a 90-day PIP with weekly check-ins.

COMPENSATION GUIDELINES

Salary Bands by Level:
- IC1 (Entry): $70,000 - $90,000
- IC2 (Mid): $90,000 - $120,000
- IC3 (Senior): $120,000 - $160,000
- IC4 (Staff): $160,000 - $200,000
- IC5 (Principal): $200,000 - $250,000

Manager Bands:
- M1 (Team Lead): $110,000 - $140,000
- M2 (Manager): $140,000 - $180,000
- M3 (Senior Manager): $180,000 - $220,000
- M4 (Director): $220,000 - $280,000

Bonus Structure:
- Individual contributor bonus: 10-15% of base salary
- Manager bonus: 15-25% of base salary
- Director+ bonus: 25-40% of base salary

HIRING PROCESS

Standard hiring timeline: 3-4 weeks from initial screen to offer

Interview Stages:
1. Recruiter screen (30 min)
2. Hiring manager interview (45 min)
3. Technical assessment or case study
4. Team interviews (3-4 people, 45 min each)
5. Final interview with department head (30 min)

Offer approval required from:
- Hiring manager
- Department head
- HR Business Partner
- CFO (for director+ roles)

HEADCOUNT PLANNING

Annual headcount budgets set during Q4 planning cycle. Mid-year adjustments require VP approval and finance review.

Each department receives quarterly headcount reports showing:
- Approved headcount
- Current headcount
- Open requisitions
- Attrition rate

TEAM RESTRUCTURING

Major org changes (affecting >5 people) require:
- Business case with rationale
- Impact analysis on affected employees
- Communication plan
- Approval from VP and Chief People Officer

REMOTE WORK POLICY

Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Full remote work requires manager and department head approval.

Remote work agreement must cover:
- Work schedule and availability hours
- Communication expectations
- Equipment and security requirements
- Performance metrics

TIME OFF POLICIES

Paid Time Off (PTO):
- 0-2 years: 15 days
- 3-5 years: 20 days
- 6+ years: 25 days
- Accrued monthly, can carry over max 5 days

Additional Leave:
- Sick leave: 10 days per year
- Parental leave: 16 weeks primary caregiver, 8 weeks secondary
- Bereavement leave: 5 days
- Jury duty: Fully paid

EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT

Pre-approval required for expenses >$500

Standard allowances:
- Team meals: $30 per person
- Client entertainment: $100 per person
- Conference attendance: $2,000 per event
- Home office setup: $1,000 one-time
- Monthly internet stipend: $75

CONFIDENTIALITY REQUIREMENTS

All managers must complete annual confidentiality training. Managers have access to sensitive employee data including:
- Salary and compensation
- Performance ratings
- Personal contact information
- Medical information (if disclosed)

Unauthorized disclosure of confidential information is grounds for immediate termination.
